üìñ Lore & Story

In the sprawling expanse of the Terran Colonial Authority, criminal syndicates have evolved beyond simple piracy and smuggling. Massive underground fortress complexes now dot inhabited worlds, housing everything from slave trafficking operations to illegal weapons manufacturing. Traditional ground forces prove inadequate against these deeply entrenched networks - by the time authorities breach surface defenses, evidence is destroyed and key targets have escaped through underground tunnels.


The Orbital Justice Division was formed as the Authority's answer to this escalating threat. Equipped with advanced space-to-surface drilling technology and real-time intelligence gathering systems, OJD warships patrol the void above colonial worlds, ready to deliver precision justice from beyond the atmosphere. No criminal sanctuary runs deep enough to escape orbital bombardment - every bunker becomes a tomb when justice descends from the stars.


Your mission: Command the OJD warship Righteous Fury in systematic enforcement operations against identified criminal networks. Each target represents years of investigation, intelligence gathering, and legal proceedings. By the time your ship enters orbit, guilt has been established - your role is to ensure consequences are delivered with surgical precision and overwhelming force.

üåä Falling Sand Physics Engine

Real-time cellular automata simulation drives terrain destruction with realistic material properties. Sand flows, concrete crumbles, metal melts, and structures collapse according to physics principles. Each material responds differently to your weaponry, requiring tactical adaptation and creating emergent destruction patterns that feel genuinely satisfying.

üè∞ Procedural Fortress Generation

Algorithmic fortress design creates unique defensive layouts with randomized layered materials, hidden air pockets containing anti-air weapons, and underground bunker networks. No two fortresses are identical, ensuring each assault requires fresh tactical thinking and approach strategies.

‚õèÔ∏è Strategic Drilling Mechanics

Multi-tiered material system with soft, medium, hard, and ultra-hard terrain types requiring different drilling approaches. üöÄ Dynamic Anti-Air Combat

Reactive defense systems emerge from hidden positions as you drill deeper, creating escalating threat levels. Surface turrets fire slow cannon projectiles while underground missile systems launch seeking projectiles. Your point defense systems must constantly adapt to changing threat patterns.

üå´Ô∏è Fog of War Exploration

Progressive target revelation through systematic destruction creates genuine exploration rewards. Hidden chambers, secret passages, and concealed defensive positions are discovered through methodical drilling. Intelligence gathering becomes part of the tactical challenge.

üí• Explosive Chain Reactions

Cascading destruction systems reward precision targeting and tactical planning. Fuel depots, ammunition stores, and structural weak points create spectacular chain reactions when properly exploited. The ultimate target's destruction provides a satisfying finale with escape sequence timing.
