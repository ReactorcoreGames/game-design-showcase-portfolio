üéØ Game Features & Mechanics

üåå

Physics-Based Ship Movement

Realistic momentum and drift mechanics create natural gameplay depth. Ships accelerate and decelerate according to physics laws, requiring players to predict and plan rather than react instantly. Automatic speed limiting and air resistance prevent overwhelming complexity while maintaining authentic zero-gravity feel that makes every maneuver meaningful.

‚ö°

Risk/Reward Charging System

Weapons charge from 1-5 seconds, delivering 5-60 damage respectively. This creates constant tactical decisions: fire quickly for guaranteed hits or risk longer exposure for devastating damage. The quarter-second firing delay adds anticipation and requires target prediction, transforming simple point-and-click into strategic timing challenges.

üîÆ

Stackable Powerup System

Six distinct weapon modifications (phase, scatter, homing, warp, triple, hypervelocity) appear every 15 seconds and stack for 60-second durations. This creates escalating tactical possibilities and ensures no two battles feel identical. Players must adapt strategies as their arsenal transforms, keeping gameplay fresh and unpredictable.

‚ú®

Cellular Automata Spectacle

Ship destruction spawns physics-driven particle systems that behave as independent cellular automata. Each ship contains unique "firework cores" that explode into spectacular displays lasting 1-5 seconds. These aren't just visual effects‚Äîthey're procedural art pieces that make every destruction feel celebratory rather than punitive.

ü™®

Dynamic Asteroid Cover

Floating asteroids provide pushable cover that splits multiple times when hit, creating evolving tactical landscapes. These environmental elements add strategic depth without overwhelming complexity, serving as both protection and physics puzzles that players can manipulate to gain advantages or create escape routes.

üéÜ

Seed-Based Victory Celebrations

Tournament victories trigger massive community fireworks displays generated from reproducible seeds. Each celebration is unique but repeatable, allowing players to revisit and share spectacular moments. The scale increases with tournament length (+30% per additional stage), ensuring greater effort yields proportionally grander celebrations.

üéÆ Game Modes

üèÜ Community Tournament Mode

Core Experience: The single game mode focuses entirely on community celebration tournaments available in three lengths: 3 stages (quick fun), 6 stages (standard experience), or 9 stages (epic celebration).


Tournament Structure: Each stage consists of a free-for-all arena battle where points accumulate based on elimination performance. The mode emphasizes friendly competition where even losing contributes to the spectacle through beautiful destruction displays.
