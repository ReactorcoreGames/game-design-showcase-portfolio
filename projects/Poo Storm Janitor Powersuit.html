Poo Storm Janitor Powersuit

Cleanup Action Game

When corporate chaos reaches legendary proportions, only one hero can save the day!


Step into the hazmat boots of a power-suited janitor tasked with cleaning up the most absurd workplace disasters imaginable. Using industrial-grade equipment and falling sand physics, transform office catastrophes into verdant gardens while battling malevolent poo monsters in this tongue-in-cheek 2D platformer.


Fast-paced, satisfying, and delightfully ridiculous 💩⚡ - because sometimes the best stress relief comes from cleaning up someone else's mess with unlimited power tools!

🎯

Target Audience

Casual gamers who appreciate absurd humor and satisfying cleanup mechanics. Perfect for players seeking stress relief through low-stakes problem-solving and those who enjoy workplace satire with a physical comedy twist.

🌟

Game's Purpose

Provides stress relief and cathartic satisfaction through empowering cleanup gameplay. Fulfills needs for competence (mastery of tools), humor (absurd scenarios), and accomplishment (transforming chaos into order).

⚡

Core Concept

Turn workplace disasters into comedic empowerment fantasies. Players wield industrial equipment to restore order from chaos, creating a sense of control and mastery over overwhelming situations through satisfying physics-based cleanup mechanics.

"In a world where corporate chaos has reached biblical proportions, one specialized industry stands between civilization and total workplace collapse."

Lore & Story

The Corporate Crisis of 2025 began when workplace stress reached a critical tipping point across the globe. Metaphorical "shitstorms" in office environments became so intense that they manifested as literal, physical poo storms that contaminated entire corporate districts.


Traditional cleaning services proved utterly inadequate against these supernatural workplace disasters. The contamination was too severe, the chaos too overwhelming, and the health hazards too extreme for ordinary humans to handle. Entire city blocks of office buildings became uninhabitable toxic zones.


From this crisis emerged a new industry: Hazmat Cleanup Specialists equipped with military-grade power armor and industrial-strength cleaning equipment. You are part of this elite force, operating out of a specialized facility that receives emergency contracts from corporations whose metaphorical problems have become very, very literal.


Your Current Mission: The Corporate Emergency Response Network has detected fresh shitstorm outbreaks across the metropolitan business district. Your company, "Absolute Cleanup Solutions", has been contracted to restore these contaminated office environments to habitability. Each successful cleanup not only saves a workplace but proves that even the most overwhelming chaos can be transformed into something beautiful 🌱 with the right tools and determination.


The fate of corporate civilization rests in your power-suited hands. Time to suit up and save the world, one office building at a time.

Controls & Interface

You control a power-suited janitor equipped with industrial cleaning tools, navigating multi-floor office buildings through platformer movement while interacting with falling sand physics and various cleaning equipment.

Control Action
A/D Move left/right through office floors
W Jump and double jump between platforms
S Drop down through one-way platforms
E Interact with equipment and level objectives
Mouse 1 Use currently selected cleaning tool
Mouse 2 Cycle tools: Powerfist, Vacuum, Mop, Water Hose

Game Layers

1Moment-to-Moment Gameplay

Fast-paced 2D platformer action where you vacuum sand poo, mop liquid poo, punch solid poo into manageable pieces, and engage in epic powerfist battles with malevolent poo monsters. Every tool interaction feels satisfying through immediate visual and physics feedback as you restore order to chaotic office environments.

2Low Metagame

9-stage campaigns with three distinct modes: Relaxing (pure cleanup), Action (poo monster fights + cleanup), and Mixed (random encounters). Each campaign features procedurally varied office layouts, poo distribution patterns, and equipment placement, ensuring fresh challenges while maintaining familiar core mechanics.

3High Metagame

You run a specialized corporate cleaning company with a portfolio of completed contracts. Each campaign represents a different client company (with satirical corporate names), and your growing reputation unlocks access to increasingly absurd workplace disasters. Earnings and completion records create a persistent sense of business growth and professional accomplishment.

4Practical Game Flow

Sessions range from 5-30 minutes per campaign, perfect for quick stress relief breaks. Launch game → Select campaign type → Choose difficulty preference → Clean 9 procedurally varied offices → View completion statistics and corporate portfolio → Either quit satisfied or start another campaign. Natural stopping points prevent addictive play patterns.

Game Features & Mechanics

🌊 Falling Sand Physics Engine

Advanced cellular automata system simulates realistic physics for three distinct poo types: sand poo (granular flow), liquid poo (fluid dynamics), and solid poo (static chunks that shatter). Each type responds uniquely to your tools and environmental forces, creating emergent cleanup challenges and satisfying cause-and-effect interactions.

🛠️ Multi-Tool Interaction System

Four specialized tools each solve specific problems: Industrial Vacuum (sand collection), Heavy-Duty Mop (liquid absorption), Powerfist Gauntlets (solid demolition + combat), and Water Hose (equipment cleaning + monster fighting). Tool switching is instant and intuitive, encouraging experimental problem-solving approaches.

🏢 Dynamic Level Generation

Every office building features procedurally arranged layouts with random distributions of mess types, equipment placement, and environmental hazards. No two cleanup jobs feel identical, yet familiar office elements (fans, water coolers, plant pots) provide consistent interactive anchors for player strategy development.

👹 Poo Monster Combat System

Malevolent creatures spawn with 100-200 HP and chaotic AI behavior that prioritizes mess-making over player targeting. Combat requires timing and positioning: close-range powerfist attacks (25 damage) or sustained water hose tactics (1 damage per shot). Monsters telegraph attacks with 2-second delays, creating tactical dodge-and-strike gameplay.

🏢 Corporate Satire Meta-Narrative

Each completed campaign adds satirical company records to your portfolio with absurd corporate names designed to amuse r/linkedinlunatics users. Earnings calculated by cleaned pixels (10 dollars each) and restored equipment (100 dollars each) provide comedic "success metrics" while maintaining the workplace disaster theme.

🎮 Replay System with Seeds

Every campaign generates with a unique seed preserved in your completion log. Replay any previous job with a sepia "flashback" filter to improve completion times or revisit particularly satisfying cleanup challenges. Perfect for speedrunning or demonstrating solutions to friends.
