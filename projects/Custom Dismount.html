üéØ Game Overview

Cathartic Dismount is a physics-based destruction game that provides safe, guilt-free stress relief through controlled virtual violence. Combining the classic appeal of Stair Dismount with advanced Toribash-style ragdoll physics, players experience satisfying cathartic release while maintaining moral clarity.


Every target is procedurally generated with an irredeemable backstory, ensuring players feel justified in their actions. The game features a unique precognition planning system inspired by Katana Zero, allowing players to preview their destruction before committing to the final, irreversible outcome.


This isn't mindless violence‚Äîit's therapeutic interaction designed to provide healthy emotional outlet in a controlled, consequence-free environment where every action serves the player's psychological well-being.

Precognition Planning System

Preview Mode: Click and drag to set initial impact vectors, launch angles, and environmental interactions. The system shows ghosted prediction trails of probable outcomes.


Finisher Targeting: During or after initial physics simulation, click on body parts to target finisher attacks. Choose from cutting, smashing, impaling, or explosive finishers with adjustable power levels.


Commitment Interface: Once satisfied with precognition preview, click "Make It Real" to execute the planned destruction. This irreversible action emphasizes the finality and weight of each decision.


Environmental Control: Simple point-and-click interaction with scenario elements like platform angles, obstacle placement, and physics object trajectories. The player controls the setup, not the individual physics calculations.

‚öôÔ∏è Game Features & Mechanics

üëÅÔ∏è

Precognition Planning System

Inspired by Katana Zero, this system lets players preview their destruction in ghosted "future sight" mode before committing to execution. The dreamlike visual filter clearly distinguishes planning from reality.


Technical Implementation: Physics simulation runs in background while player adjusts parameters. Visual ghosting shows probability cones for impact zones, trajectory paths, and potential finisher opportunities. Once committed, the precognition becomes irreversible reality.


This system ensures every destruction feels meaningful and deliberate rather than accidental, maximizing the cathartic impact of each decision.

‚öñÔ∏è

Moral Justification Framework

Drawing inspiration from Watch Dogs profiler system, every target receives procedurally generated backstory detailing their irredeemable actions and harmful intentions, eliminating player guilt.


Target Generation: Each person has randomized name, appearance colors, belief system, past crimes they're proud of, and future harmful plans. Examples: "Plans to scam elderly out of retirement funds" or "Believes disabled people deserve to suffer."


This framework transforms destruction from senseless violence into righteous justice, providing guilt-free cathartic release.

üé≠

Toribash-Style Ragdoll Physics

Advanced ragdoll system featuring realistic dismemberment, detailed joint physics, and visceral impact feedback. Bodies react authentically to forces while maintaining primitive geometric aesthetic.


Physics Details: Individual limb tracking, realistic bone breaking thresholds, momentum conservation, and environmental interaction. Blood and dismemberment effects provide satisfying visual feedback without excessive gore.


The physics system emphasizes weighty, consequential impacts that make each destruction feel significant and cathartic.

üé≤

Dynamic Scenario Generation

Procedural system creates infinite scenario variations using modular environmental components. Train impacts, stair falls, machinery collisions, and traffic accidents with randomized elements.


Generation Logic: Scenario templates (train platform, stairway, industrial machinery) receive randomized components (train shape variations, stair materials, machinery types) and target positioning for guaranteed impact while maintaining variety.


Ensures fresh cathartic experiences without repetitive content consumption, supporting long-term stress relief utility.

‚öîÔ∏è

Cinematic Finisher System

Inspired by Metal Gear Rising: Revengeance, slow-motion finisher mode allows precise final strikes with camera effects and impact enhancement. Choose cutting angles, smash directions, or explosive timing.


Finisher Types: Katana precision cuts, buster sword smashes, kinetic force punches, physics projectile impacts, explosive body part destruction, and impalement attacks. Each with adjustable power levels and targeting options.


The finisher system provides ultimate cathartic climax with permanent consequences, emphasizing the finality of justice served.

üî∑

Minimalist Aesthetic

Pure primitive shapes and geometric forms create maximum impact through simplicity. No textures or complex models‚Äîjust effective physics interaction with bold, clean visual design.


Visual Philosophy: Following original Stair Dismount principles, geometric primitives allow player imagination to fill details while physics and sound design provide emotional weight. Simple forms ensure focus on interaction rather than visual complexity.


Minimalist approach maximizes accessibility while ensuring smooth performance across all devices and clear visual communication of physics interactions.

üé≤ Development Trivia

  • Finnish Origins: Direct homage to the legendary Finnish games "Porrasturvat" (Stair Dismount) and "Rekkaturvat" (Truck Dismount) by tAAt, which won Assembly demo competitions and pioneered physics-based destruction gaming in 2002-2003.
  • Psychology-First Design: The moral justification system was implemented specifically to address the psychological discomfort many players feel with virtual violence, transforming potential guilt into righteous satisfaction.
  • Precognition Inspiration: The planning system directly references Katana Zero's time manipulation mechanics, but applies the concept to physics prediction rather than temporal rewind, maintaining causality and consequence weight.
  • Cathartic Gaming Research: Game design based on established psychological research showing that controlled virtual violence can provide healthy stress relief when combined with moral framework and meaningful choice systems.
