📖 Lore & Story

The Year 2347 - The Great Reclamation Era


Humanity has inherited a planet layered with the compressed remnants of industrial civilization. Beneath sprawling cities like Neo-Geneva and New Singapore lie vast geological strata of "lithified landfill" - ancient waste deposits transformed by catalytic processes into valuable crystalline materials.


You are a Licensed Cascade Technician, operating humanity's most sophisticated material reclamation technology. The Collidotron processing chambers represent the pinnacle of 23rd-century physics engineering - precision machines capable of transforming environmental burdens into construction materials for humanity's expansion to the stars.


The Mission: Each crystalline slab contains compressed layers of plastic polymers, metallic composites, and organic compounds - the buried mistakes of previous centuries now serving as the raw materials for sustainable futures. Your expertise in cascade physics directly contributes to humanity's greatest environmental restoration project, turning the planet's industrial scars into the foundation of interstellar civilization.


Master the ancient art of controlled demolition to heal a world and build the next chapter of human expansion.

⚙️ Controls & Interface

Control Description
Mouse Button 1 (Primary) Initial tile spark to start cascade, or enter slow-motion mode for precise trajectory adjustment and ball redirection.
Mouse Button 2 (Secondary) Toggle 1.5x time acceleration for faster cascade observation, overriding any slow-motion effects.
Strategic Intervention Pause time, select any active ball, then click to redirect it along new trajectory for optimal cascade optimization.
Precision Machine Interface Clean 32×19 grid with titanium frame, minimal HUD design emphasizing the beautiful physics rather than complex menus.

🏗️ Game Layers

1Moment-to-Moment Gameplay

Strategic tile selection to initiate cascades, precision trajectory planning for optimal efficiency, and tactical slow-motion interventions to guide reaction orbs through complex crystalline lattices. Players experience immediate satisfaction from beautiful physics while building mastery of cascade optimization techniques.

2Low Metagame - Loaf Processing

Each campaign processes one complete loaf of lithified material, sliced into 9 distinct slabs with varying sizes (16×9, 24×13, 32×18) and unique material properties. Progress through diverse cascade challenges with different quirks like magnetic fields, quantum instability, or volatile compounds, culminating in beautiful 3×3 gallery completion.

3High Metagame - Processing Archive

Persistent archive of all processed loaves with procedurally generated names, complete cascade statistics, and seed-based replay capability. Discover increasingly anomalous materials with exotic physics behaviors as your expertise grows, building a personal catalog of industrial beauty and environmental restoration achievements.

4Practical Game Flow

Quick session setup with immediate slab loading → Single-click cascade initiation → Meditative observation with optional strategic interventions → Efficiency analysis and pattern appreciation → Seamless archive integration with natural stopping points that respect player time and attention.

🎮 Game Modes

⚗️ Standard Processing

Core campaign experience processing regular lithified slabs with efficiency tracking and cascade optimization. Perfect for both quick 5-minute sessions and longer strategic deep-dives into complex material configurations.

🔬 Anomalous Materials

🔧 Game Features & Mechanics

⚡

Physics-Based Chain Reactions

Authentic cascade physics with consistent velocity maintenance and realistic collision dynamics. Reaction orbs maintain fixed speeds while exhibiting natural bounce behaviors, creating predictable yet beautiful emergent patterns that reward strategic thinking and physics intuition.

⏱️

Strategic Intervention System

Unique slow-motion mechanics allowing precise trajectory adjustment of active cascade orbs. Players can pause time, select any moving ball, and redirect its path to optimize efficiency or access difficult areas - bridging strategic planning with zen observation.

🧱

Modular Material Properties

Different tile materials exhibit unique breaking behaviors and orb interactions. Steel tiles require multiple hits but produce penetrating balls, while compound tiles shatter easily but create standard orbs. Material variety creates strategic depth and visual diversity.

🎨

Procedural Slab Generation

Kaleidoscopic and fractal patterns generated through sophisticated algorithms, ensuring every slab offers unique visual beauty and cascade challenges. Pockets of empty space create strategic routing opportunities while maintaining aesthetic coherence.

📈

Campaign Progression System

Structured progression through loaves containing 9 slabs each, with material quirks and size variations creating natural difficulty curves. Complete loaves are archived as 3×3 galleries, providing long-term collection goals and visual achievement records.

💾

Archive & Replay System

Seed-based cascade reproduction allows exact replay of any previous processing session. Study your techniques, share interesting configurations, and experiment with alternative approaches to previously completed slabs for continuous learning and mastery development.

🛠️ Technical Excellence

High-Performance Physics: Optimized to support up to 600 simultaneous reaction orbs with consistent collision detection and realistic bounce dynamics, ensuring smooth cascade experiences even in complex scenarios.


Efficient Visual Design: Elegant sprite-based graphics with color tinting systems for material variety, complemented by tasteful particle effects for tile breaking and orb trails. Minimal yet impactful visual feedback that emphasizes the beauty of physics over flashy distractions.


Modular Content System: Systematic asset reuse through procedural generation and color variation, allowing infinite visual variety from efficient development resources while maintaining consistent aesthetic quality and performance standards.
