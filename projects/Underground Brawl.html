Underground Brawl

Ragdoll Combat

Step into the underground fighting scene where physics meets strategy in visceral hand-to-hand combat. Control ragdoll fighters in dimly lit venues, using realistic collision mechanics to outmaneuver opponents.


Every punch matters, every bruise tells a story, and every knockout is earned through tactical positioning rather than button mashing. This is fighting where your brain matters as much as your reflexes.


Pure fist combat with physics-based consequences - no weapons, no gimmicks, just you, your opponent, and the laws of motion.

🎯 Target Audience

Fighting game enthusiasts who appreciate strategic depth, physics simulation fans seeking realistic combat mechanics, and players looking for cathartic stress relief through controlled competition.

🎪 Game's Purpose

Provides healthy stress release through competitive combat, develops tactical thinking and spatial awareness, offers mastery-based progression that rewards skill over button combinations.

⚡ Core Concept

Traditional fighting game enhanced with realistic ragdoll physics, where strategic positioning and limb management create deeper tactical gameplay than conventional fighting mechanics.

Lore & Story

In the sprawling urban landscape, beneath the polished surface of corporate buildings and busy streets, lies a network of underground venues where people gather to reclaim something essential that modern life has stripped away - the primal satisfaction of physical challenge and genuine human connection.


The Underground Circuit exists in basements, abandoned warehouses, converted parking garages, and forgotten spaces throughout the city. These aren't criminal enterprises or illegal operations - they're consensual fighting clubs where stressed office workers, frustrated laborers, restless students, and anyone feeling disconnected from their own vitality can step into the ring.


Every participant signs waivers, follows safety protocols, and enters with mutual respect. The goal isn't to harm or humiliate - it's to feel alive again. To test yourself against another person in the most fundamental way humans have always resolved tension: through controlled, respectful combat.


You play as fighters navigating this underground world, seeking venues that match your skill level and fighting style. Some locations cater to beginners learning to channel their frustration productively. Others host seasoned practitioners who've turned stress relief into an art form. All share the same core principle: consensual combat as a path to personal wellness and authentic human connection.

Controls & Interface

🕹️ Mouse + Keyboard Combat System

Intuitive physics-based control scheme that puts you in direct command of your fighter's movement and actions:

Control Action
M1 (Left Click) Move toward cursor position
M2 (Right Click) Rotate to face cursor direction
E / M4 Throw punches (both hands)
W / Shift / Spacebar Boost acceleration toward cursor
Mouse Wheel Zoom in/out (triggers slow motion)

Combat Mechanics: Your ragdoll fighter responds to physics - momentum affects your strikes, positioning determines effectiveness, and damaged limbs reduce your defensive options. Master the interplay between movement, timing, and spatial awareness.

"When your left arm goes numb, you better hope your right hook still works"

Game Layers

1.Moment-to-Moment Gameplay

Real-time physics combat where every collision matters. Manage your fighter's limb health while strategically positioning for strikes. Damaged arms and legs become liabilities, forcing you to adapt your fighting style mid-match. Timing, spacing, and physics momentum determine victory or defeat.

2.Low Metagame

Progress through tournament brackets and ranking systems. Each victory earns reputation points and unlocks new underground venues. Build your fighter's legacy through consistent performance across multiple fights, with each match contributing to your overall standing in the underground circuit.

3.High Metagame

Establish dominance across the entire underground fighting network. Unlock exclusive venues, face legendary opponents, and build a reputation that opens doors to the most prestigious (and dangerous) fighting circuits. Your achievements become part of underground fighting folklore.

4.Practical Game Flow

Quick 3-5 minute matches for casual stress relief, or extended tournament runs for deep engagement. Natural break points between fights allow for real-life interruptions, while tournament modes provide structured long-form challenges that can span multiple gaming sessions.

Game Modes

🏆 Tournament Mode

Classic bracket-style progression through underground fighting circuits. Face increasingly skilled opponents as you advance through elimination rounds. Each tournament has its own venue theme and difficulty curve, providing structured challenges with clear victory conditions.

⚡ Quick Fight

🎯 Training Mode

Hone your skills against AI opponents with adjustable behavior patterns. Practice advanced techniques, experiment with positioning strategies, and master the physics system at your own pace. Includes replay analysis and move practice tools.

Game Features & Mechanics

🤖Ragdoll Physics Combat

Every fighter is a fully articulated ragdoll with realistic joint constraints and momentum physics. Strikes create genuine physical reactions - no canned animations or predetermined outcomes. The physics engine ensures every fight feels unique and organic, with emergent combat situations arising naturally from the interaction of two physics-based characters.

💪Limb Damage System

Individual body parts take damage and become progressively less effective. Bruised arms can't guard as well or throw punches with full force. Damaged legs affect mobility and stance stability. This creates evolving tactical situations where both fighters must adapt their strategies as the fight progresses and their capabilities change.

🎯Strategic Positioning

Victory comes from understanding angles, distance, and timing rather than memorizing combos. Use physics momentum to your advantage - a well-timed dodge can set up a devastating counter-strike. Master the art of creating openings through superior positioning and spatial awareness.

✂️Character Customization

Express your fighting personality through diverse hairstyle options and vibrant hair colors. All customization is purely cosmetic - every fighter has identical capabilities, ensuring victories are earned through skill rather than character stats. Create a look that intimidates opponents or reflects your fighting spirit.

🏠Underground Venues

Fight in atmospheric locations that capture the gritty essence of underground combat. From basement clubs to abandoned warehouses, each venue has its own distinct mood and crowd energy. Environmental storytelling through venue design adds immersion to every bout.

🧠Physics-Based Strategy

Success requires understanding momentum, leverage, and collision mechanics. A fighter moving backward takes less impact from frontal strikes, while forward momentum can turn a glancing blow into a knockout punch. Master these physical principles to gain tactical advantages over opponents.

"It's not about hitting hard - it's about hitting smart when your opponent can't guard anymore"

Fun Combat Trivia

  • Pure Physics Creates Unique Stories: No two fights are identical due to the realistic physics engine. Even fighting the same opponent twice will produce completely different combat dynamics and memorable moments.
  • Cosmetic Equality: All customization is purely visual - every fighter has identical stats and capabilities. Victory comes from player skill and tactical thinking, not character advantages or equipment bonuses.
  • Underground Atmosphere: Each venue features its own crowd behavior and ambient details that react to fight intensity. The audience becomes part of the experience, cheering for spectacular moves and gasping at brutal impacts.
