üìñ Lore & Story

Helios Sector: Set within humanity's expanding colonial sphere in the smaller region of the Milky Way in the Orion Arm, encompassing our original Sol system and approximately fifty colonized star systems. Connected by a network of jumpgates, these colonies represent humanity's first major expansion beyond Earth and the Solar System, with no faster-than-light travel available beyond these crucial gate networks that were set up the old fashion way.


The Ideological Fracture: Following humanity's discovery of initial alien species and the revolutionary genetic transformation technology, two irreconcilable worldviews emerged. Years of growing tensions between domination-hierarchy minded factions and collaboration-freedom oriented groups have reached a breaking point. The introduction of genetic enhancement that could fundamentally alter humanity itself became the final catalyst for war.


Total War Declaration: Tensions have boiled over to the point of existential warfare where only one side will survive. Both factions command equal military strength, identical technology, and the same devastating Mechs and massive Arms Forts. With a deliberate genetic metamorphosis virus actively already dividing humanity, complete eradication of the enemy becomes the only way to stop the change.


The Stakes: Billions of lives hang in the balance as regional campaigns determine access to civilian megashelters. Each battlefield victory grants the winner a step closer to methodically exterminate enemy populations without mercy - turning war into overkill genocide until one side ceases to exist entirely across the colonial space of the Helios Sector.

üèóÔ∏è Game Architecture

1 Moment-to-Moment Combat

Core Gameplay Loop: Intense mecha-vs-mecha skirmishes where every pilot has equal capabilities. Master advanced movement techniques like boost-dashing, aerial maneuvering, and form transformation while managing weapon loadouts, ammunition, and tactical positioning.

Combat Depth: No fall damage, momentum-based physics, and environmental interaction create a three-dimensional battlefield. Weapon weight affects movement speed, reload times impact vulnerability windows, and battlefield hazards add unpredictable elements.

2 Battle Campaign Structure

Token-Based Warfare: Each battle depletes a finite pool of combatants (100-1000 tokens depending on map scale). Victory requires systematically eliminating enemy forces while preserving your own through superior tactics and individual skill.

Dynamic Battlefield Events: Massive Arms Forts traverse predetermined routes, devastating enemy emplacements while providing resupply opportunities. Weather hazards, supply drops, and background fleet warfare create evolving combat conditions.

3 Strategic Campaign Progression

Regional Conquest System: Five major regions each containing six battles (5 standard + 1 fortress assault). Victory in regional campaigns unlocks population centers for systematic elimination, directly impacting enemy civilization strength.

Escalating Consequences: Each regional victory grants access to defenseless civilian megashelters where players can methodically exterminate enemy populations. Campaign choices determine whether civilizations survive, flee, or face complete annihilation.

4 Session Flow Design

Seamless Entry/Exit: Launch directly into combat with customizable session lengths (5 minutes to 2+ hours). Save states allow pausing at any intermission screen without progress loss.

Performance Tracking: Comprehensive statistics including kill streaks, weapon proficiency, mobility achievements, and civilian elimination counts. Generate randomized "military honors" based on campaign performance for comedic relief after intense warfare.
