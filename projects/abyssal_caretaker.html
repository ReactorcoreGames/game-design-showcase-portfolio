Lore & Story

üèõÔ∏è The Ancient Covenant

Long before humanity discovered the ocean's depths, the Abyssal Caretakers maintained a sacred covenant with the planetary water system. These amphibious guardians, evolved from the deepest trenches, served as living immune systems for the ocean - detecting, isolating, and neutralizing threats to marine equilibrium.

‚ö° The Great Forgetting

As human civilization expanded, the surface world forgot the ancient partnership that once existed between land and sea. Industrial pollution began overwhelming the caretakers' ability to heal, forcing most into deep hibernation in thermal vent sanctuaries. The ocean's health declined rapidly without its guardians' active protection.

üö¢ The Container Crisis

The final catastrophe arrived with the shipping container era. Thousands of metal boxes carrying unknown chemicals now litter the ocean floor, their contents slowly poisoning entire ecosystems. The crisis has reached critical mass - even the deepest abyssal plains show signs of contamination that threaten all marine life.

üåä Your Awakening

You are the last caretaker to emerge from the dying thermal vents, awakening to find an ocean in unprecedented crisis. Your mission is both clear and desperate: rise through the five oceanic zones, restore what can be saved, and establish a new balance between human activity and ocean health before complete ecosystem collapse occurs.

üî± The Surface Revelation

The journey upward will reveal the true scope of humanity's impact on the ocean, but also their potential for change. Your actions in the depths create ripple effects that reach the surface world - can you bridge two civilizations that have forgotten they share the same planet?

Game Layers

üåä Moment-to-Moment Gameplay

Side-scrolling through polluted underwater landscapes, using mouse-aimed tools to clean contamination, platforming through wreckage fields, and salvaging container technology. Each area presents 2D environmental puzzles that require the right tool combinations and suit upgrades to solve.

üó∫Ô∏è Zone-Based Progression (Low Metagame)

Progress through five distinct oceanic environments: Abyssal Plains ‚Üí Continental Slope ‚Üí Continental Shelf ‚Üí Coastal Waters ‚Üí Surface Ports. Each zone requires specific suit upgrades and presents unique pollution challenges, from deep-sea container graveyards to toxic surface oil slicks.

üåç Ocean Health Persistence (High Metagame)

Areas you clean stay clean and continue to recover over time. Return to previously restored zones to see thriving marine life, natural healing processes, and new opportunities for deeper environmental work. Your actions create a living, breathing ocean recovery system.

‚è±Ô∏è Practical Game Flow

Sessions begin with suit diagnostics and zone selection. Spend 30-90 minutes exploring, cleaning pollution, and gathering upgrade materials. Natural stopping points occur when completing zone restoration goals or achieving major suit upgrades, leaving you with clear progress and anticipation for the next depth.

‚ö†Ô∏è Environmental Crisis Facts

  • Over 10,000 shipping containers are lost at sea annually, many containing hazardous materials that leach into marine ecosystems
  • Calcium carbide (featured in the MSC Elsa 3 incident) reacts with seawater to produce highly flammable acetylene gas, creating underwater fire hazards
  • Microplastics have been found in the deepest ocean trenches, including the Mariana Trench at depths exceeding 10,000 meters
  • Chemical spills at 50-meter depths can reach coastal ecosystems within days, while abyssal contamination persists for centuries
  • Deep-sea mining and shipping pollution threaten chemosynthetic communities that exist nowhere else on Earth
  • The game's five-zone depth system mirrors real oceanic pollution layers, from surface oil slicks to abyssal sediment contamination
