Lore & Story

Set in the early 1990s, you play as SkullHead - once an ordinary young American man of mixed heritage who endured life's worst cruelties. Unloved by parents, bullied at school, abused at work, manipulated by friends, victimized by criminals, and crushed by corrupt systems, his accumulated suffering became an all-consuming hatred.


In his darkest moment, he made the ultimate bargain - trading his soul to the Devil in exchange for power and immortality. His transformation stripped away his humanity, leaving only a skeletal skull atop a spine where his head once was, forever marking him as the Devil's instrument of vengeance.


Now, SkullHead roams American towns and cities as an immortal agent of chaos, hunting society's worst people with supernatural guarantee of victory. His mission is not heroism but cathartic revenge - to systematically eliminate the bullies, abusers, and corrupt individuals who make life hell for others. The Devil's contract ensures he always wins, eventually, making every location he targets inevitably fall to his brutal justice.

Game Layers

1

Moment-to-Moment Gameplay

Physics-based ragdoll combat using chainsaws, knives, axes, and American firearms. Strategic dismemberment targeting to disable different enemy types efficiently. Dark magical health regeneration through eliminating enemies - the devil's contract rewards aggressive play by instantly restoring any damage when you kill or dismember opponents. Environmental destruction using randomly-placed destructible props throughout levels.

2

Low Metagame (Location Elimination)

Target specific buildings and places for revenge through 5-15 procedurally generated levels. You type in the actual name of the real place you want to attack, then choose from location presets like office buildings, schools, malls, or restaurants. The devil's contract ensures checkpoints - death simply rewinds time until victory is achieved, making failure impossible.

3

High Metagame (Death Journal)

Persistent revenge map showing towns and city areas you're systematically targeting. Features a Doom (1993) style intermission screen with blood splats marking eliminated locations and a "You are here" indicator showing your next target. You enter real town and city names to create your personal revenge geography. Completed locations can be replayed but are marked as "canonically eliminated."

4

Practical Game Flow

Game Features & Mechanics

1

Dynamic Dismemberment Combat

Strategic body part targeting with realistic blood spray physics and limb separation mechanics. Each enemy type has different weak points and dismemberment strategies. Arms can be severed to disable weapon use, legs removed to limit mobility, and head removal for instant elimination. The physics system makes every impact feel meaningful and visceral.

2

Devil's Contract Power System

Death triggers supernatural time rewind to previous checkpoint - Devil's contract ensures you always eventually succeed. Additionally, eliminating or dismembering enemies instantly restores any damage you've suffered through dark magical regeneration, rewarding aggressive play and making retreat unnecessary.

3

Deadly Arsenal System

Chainsaws for close brutal work with revving mechanics and blood spray effects. Knives and axes for silent precision takedowns and throwing attacks. American firearms including pistols, shotguns, and rifles for ranged elimination. Each weapon type excels in different tactical situations and enemy encounters.

4

Destructible Environment Library

Randomly placed interactive props including desks, lockers, vending machines, copy machines, and office furniture that can be destroyed, weaponized, or used for cover. Environmental destruction creates dynamic battlefields and additional tactical options during combat encounters.

5

Personal Death Journal System

Create your own geography of revenge by naming real places you want to see Skullhead eliminate. The system generates appropriate level layouts based on your chosen location type (office, school, mall, etc.) while preserving the personal emotional connection that makes each elimination feel meaningful and cathartic.

6

Societal Enemy Archetypes

Different enemy types representing various levels of society's worst people - from workplace bullies to corrupt executives, school administrators to mall security guards. Each archetype has unique AI behaviors, weapon preferences, and dismemberment vulnerabilities that require different tactical approaches to eliminate effectively.
