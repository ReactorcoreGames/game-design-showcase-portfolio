üöÄ Lore & Story

The 22nd Century Reality: Humanity's expansion across known space has created a complex web of interplanetary politics, corporate conflicts, and territorial disputes. Traditional military forces struggle with the vast distances and diverse environmental challenges that define modern space warfare. Professional contractor units have emerged to fill the critical gaps in capability and political flexibility.


The Starforge Legion: Established as an elite contractor organization, the Starforge Legion specializes in high-risk operations across multiple stellar environments. Using advanced transformable mecha technology, Legion pilots handle assignments that require both the speed of space fighters and the tactical versatility of ground-based units.


Your Role: As a certified Legion contractor, you operate with full professional autonomy across assigned sectors. Your missions range from orbital facility security to asteroid mining protection, from planetary surface reconnaissance to deep space interdiction. Each contract tests different aspects of mecha operation in the unforgiving conditions of space.


The Challenge: Every environment presents unique tactical requirements - zero gravity combat in asteroid fields, atmospheric entry operations on hostile worlds, high-velocity interceptions in open space. Success demands mastery of both mechanical systems and environmental adaptation, where small mistakes have lethal consequences.
